Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some common questions about YunaraX402.

What is YunaraX402?

YunaraX402 is a multi-chain launchpad token analyzer that helps you discover and analyze new cryptocurrency tokens across Solana, Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Base. We provide AI-powered insights, real-time trading data, and KOL tracking to help you make informed trading decisions.

How does AI analysis work?

Yunara, our AI Trading Assistant, uses Moralis AI to analyze token data, market metrics, holder distribution, and security scores. It provides comprehensive risk assessment, trading recommendations, and detailed insights about each token. AI analysis requires 1 Compute Unit from your active plan via the x402 protocol.

Which blockchains are supported?

YunaraX402 currently supports Solana, Ethereum, BNB Chain (BSC), and Base Network. You can search for tokens, view trending tokens, and analyze tokens from any of these chains. The platform automatically detects which blockchain a token belongs to based on its contract address format.

How do I get free AI analyses?

Complete quests in the Quest Dashboard! Follow our Twitter (@yunaraX402), like a post, and comment on one post to unlock 10 free AI analyses. You can check your quest progress and claim rewards from your user dashboard.

Is my wallet information secure?

Yes! When you log in with Google, we create encrypted wallets for you. Private keys are encrypted using AES-256-CBC encryption and stored securely. You can use these wallets to pay for AI analyses without connecting external wallets like MetaMask or Phantom.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept USDC payments via the x402 protocol on Base, Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Solana networks. Payments are processed on-chain and verified automatically. You can fund your wallet directly from the Wallet Dashboard.

How often is token data updated?

Token data is cached and updated regularly. Price data, volume, and holder counts are refreshed frequently. For the most up-to-date information, you can manually refresh data by clicking the refresh button or reloading the page.
